FAQs section

How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, go to our homepage and use the search bar. Enter your travel dates, departure and destination cities, and any other preferences. Our system will show you available flights that match your criteria.

Can I change my booking later?

Yes, you can usually make changes to your booking. Visit the "Manage Booking" section on our website, enter your booking details, and follow the instructions to make necessary changes.

What's the baggage allowance?

You can check in online through our website. Just visit the "Check-In" page, enter your booking details, and follow the steps to complete your check-in and get your boarding pass.

What happens if my flight is delayed or canceled?

In case of delays or cancellations, we'll do our best to inform you in advance. You can check your flight status on our website under the "Flight Status" section.

Can I request a refund if I need to cancel my flight?

Refund policies vary. Review our "Cancellation and Refund" page for information on whether your booking is eligible for a refund.

Are there discounts for children or infants?

Yes, we often offer discounted fares for children and infants. Check the booking page for options and prices.
